Detailed Description

Traffic light state, including the phase's absolute and relative interval length if supported by the traffic light

Member Enumeration Documentation

enum ibeo::common::sdk::TrafficLightStateIn3600::Phase : uint8_t
strong

Representation of all possible traffic light phases.

Enumerator
Red 

Traffic light signals "red".

RedAmber 

Traffic light signals "red" and "amber". Just after "red" phase, presumably only in Germany.

Amber 

Traffic light signals "amber". After "green" phase.

Green 

Traffic light signals "green".

Off 

Traffic light signals nothing or "amber" blinking.
